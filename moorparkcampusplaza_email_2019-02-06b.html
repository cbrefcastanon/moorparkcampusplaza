Moorpark Campus Plaza offers an investor attractive In Place Income from a wide range of tenants which includes corporate leases with Starbucks and McDonalds (Dark) as well as Substantial Upside Potential through lease up of vacant spaces (10% of GLA) and renewal/ conversion of certain ground level leased spaces (14,822 SF) from gross to NNN leases.

Campus Plaza benefits from its location in Moorpark, CA, an affluent Southern California city in Ventura County offering an Average HH income of +/-$112,235 within a three (3) mile radius, proximity to the 118 Fwy./Collins Drive interchange (+/-88,000 VPD)* and the Moorpark Community College (+/-19,000 Students and Staff Members).
